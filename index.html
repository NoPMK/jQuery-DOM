A Very serious guide to

Ezreal

Ezreal was a cheerful explorer of the unkown, yearning for knowledge and treasures.
He is an adc/apc champion with crazy high mobility.
All of his abilities are skillshots which means he is quite fun to play but takes years to master.
Too bad Riot fucking ruined him and now he looks and moves like a retarded supporting actor from some low budget anime.

Skills

  • Passive: Rising Spell Force
      • Gaining Bitching Power for each spell that hits an enemy stacking to infinity and beyond.
  • Q: Mystic Shot
      • Ezreal fires a harmless bolt of anime tears watering the first enemy it hits.
  • W: Essence Flux
      • Ezreal shoots an orb of adherence which overly attaches to the first enemy it hits. The orb explodes and deals 1 damage if Ezreal hits it with a damaging spell.
  • E: Arcane Shift
      • Ezreal teleports to a worse position than before and fires Homer from The Simpsons to the furthest enemy. Prioritizing enemies with full health.
  • R: Trueshot Barrage
      • Ezreal winds up for 10 mins before firing a 1x1 pixel ray of fart dealing absolutely minimal damage to each enemy it passes through.
